Zarna

Zarna is a passionate designer with excellent communication and
presentation skill.With the experience of 17 years in the field of textile
designing, she believes in strong work ethics and discipline along with
innovative thinking.

She has worked with almost all leading export and domestic brands
and has deep understanding of global market and forecasting woven
fabrics for men and women for relevant markets.




















Graduated from one of the India’s premier engineering college, Zarna has gained deep knowledge of fabric designing with woven technics. She had started her career as a designer at
B.V.M. (Textile division of Sintex industries Ltd.),enhanced her knowledge withfusion of technical & creative designing. At Ashima Ltd., she worked for almost more than seven years, for
almost all the major brands across the globe. Lastly, she was heading Design Studio at B.V.M. (Textile division of Sintex industries Ltd.). Her versatility and ability to work with people from
diverse background and all level of management added flavour to her work.

She had designed successful seasonal fabric collections for Men and Women at B.V.M. and presented to various exports and domestic brands. Vision to work towards trend forecast and
market requirement aspects has made her collection useful for the designers.

She believes in spreading knowledge and creating designers for tomorrow’s world. She had guided students from leading fashion and design institutes like NID, NIFT, LDCE, Vanasthali
institute, UID etc.
